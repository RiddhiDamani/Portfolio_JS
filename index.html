About Me

My name is Riddhi Damani. I'm creatively inclined Coder. My passion for arts and coding have truly pushed me into combining both the sides of the world together - the art of design with the art of coding. In past few months, I've built out personal projects depicting my interest in Responsive Web Designing/Development, JavaScript & HTML5 Canvas 2D Games, and Android Apps development using Android Studio IDE. When I'm not pushing commits, I'm probably sketching/doodling.

I earned Bachelor Degree in Information Technology from a reputed engineering institute in Mumbai. I have 4.7 years of professional experience in IT Industry working for top-notch companies like Infosys, TCS in their Banking & Financial sector - majorly focusing on development/maintenance and support of mainframe applications at the backend. Also, I have a year of experience as SEO (Search Engine Optimization) Content Writer where I gained knowledge about google optimization techniques, keyword stuffing, blog posting.

In my last job, I have worked with Ericsson as a User Interface developer and realized my desire to pursue my interest in web/mobile application development. Currently, I am pursuing my master's degree in computer science - majoring in system and software development and minoring in human-computer interaction.
